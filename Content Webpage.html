Ego Typing

It's an accepted fact that the world runs with knowledge as the source of all initiatives. After all, knowledge acts as building blocks to found a structure, whether it be families, institutions, corporations, or governments. A competitive, tiring, yet exhilarating cycle of trials is what binds every human being altogether, all for the pursuit of the invaluable commodity. In this race, many Atlas have been crafted to carry the weight of the world for others to fluorish. They are often associated as guardians of knowledge, safeguarding the worth of intellect for generations to come.



As of now, I am one of the many in-training strong-willed students of life that absorbs every bit of knowledge like a sponge. I have bathed myself in my own blood, seat, and tears braving through the dense jungle of intellectual hardships. Through those experiences, I have learned many skills and ideas that carry weight on the world. Maturity really comes with age, yet the journey truly ends with my demise. There are still more skills to learn, acquire, and practice. There are also many tales of wisdom awaiting me in the game of life, so may Allah support me in my pursuit of excellence. This is me, myself, and I, Mohammad Hafiz Irfan bin Sharizal.
